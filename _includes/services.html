What are we about?.

SAiDl (Society for Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning) is a non-profit professional organization which works on artificial intelligence and deep learning. This organization consists of enthusiasts who work on applications of data science in Computer Vision, Natural Language Programming, time series and artificial general intelligence. To promote Deep learning and Artificial Intelligence and setting standards for the ethical professional practice of data science.

-Building a group of members who are passionate about data science. We plan to achieve by conducting a detailed series of lectures on various aspects of AI

-Inculcating the spirit of research to improve knowledge base of its members

-Working in real-life projects

-Forming an alumni network who are in the field of deep learning

-Collaborating with industries to improve our technical know-how
